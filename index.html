Who am I?

Well I guess you already know my name, but that's not nearly enough to answer this question. I'm a currently a junior studying Computer Science and minoring in Math at Purdue University - Boiler Up! I chose Computer Science as my major in order to play a part with the technology that's so integrated into our everyday lives. I want to be able to make the divide between humans and technology more integrated than having to interact with a 2-D screen. The person I look up to the most is Elon Musk because of his willingness to make the world a better place with an abundant amount of ambition despite the odds. I love creating websites and android apps, hiking, going out on impromptu adventures, reading books, and watching cute videos of cats.
